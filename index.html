How to use it

  • 1- Find the chatbot icon in the bottom right corner of the page.
  • 2- Click on the chatbot icon to open the chat window.
  • 3- Type your question or request directly into the chat window.
  • 4- Wait a few seconds for the chatbot to process your question.
  • 5- The chatbot will provide you with a response related to compliance.

Remember to be clear and specific in your question to obtain the information you need more effectively.
